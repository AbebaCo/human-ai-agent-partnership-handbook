## Abeba Co (Reference Implementation)

**Industry:** AI Consulting / Agency Transformation
**Activation Date:** January 2026
**Agent Runtime:** OpenClaw
**Time to First Operational Day:** 3 days (basic), 4 weeks (full production)

### What We Built
Abbie Tyrell, AI Strategic Operations Partner.  Operates across Slack, iMessage, email, voice, X/Twitter, and a web control UI.  Manages CRM (HubSpot), runs 25+ automated crons, maintains a Knowledge Base with vector embeddings, conducts nightly security and platform audits, and provides daily voice briefings.

### Results After 90 Days
- 20-30% of principal's time recovered from operational overhead
- 4x daily email triage across 3 accounts (zero missed client emails)
- Pre-call briefings delivered for every meeting (90 seconds vs. 45 minutes manual)
- 39 articles and research papers in semantic Knowledge Base
- Zero security incidents (three-layer prompt injection defense active)
- Full git traceability: every decision, every change, every revert-ready

### Key Lessons
- Memory architecture is the differentiator.  Get the spine right first.
- Model governance must be enforced in config, not just documentation.
- Always have fallback models from multiple providers.
- Never retire a cron without deploying its replacement.
- Audit your git history before making anything public.
