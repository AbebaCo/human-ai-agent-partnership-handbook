# Contributing

## Share Your Activation Story

If you have activated a Human | AI Agent Partnership using this handbook (or any variant of it), we want to feature your experience.

### How to Contribute

1. **Fork this repository**
2. **Add your story to `CASE_STUDIES.md`** using the template below
3. **Submit a pull request**

### Case Study Template

```markdown
## [Your Company Name]

**Industry:** [Your industry]
**Activation Date:** [Month Year]
**Agent Runtime:** [OpenClaw / other]
**Time to First Operational Day:** [e.g., 5 days]

### What We Built
[Describe your agent: name, role, channels, key capabilities]

### Results After 30 Days
[Metrics: time saved, tasks automated, cost comparison]

### Key Lessons
[What surprised you, what you would do differently]

### Contact
[Optional: name, email, or social handle]
```

### Other Ways to Contribute

- **Bug reports:** If you find errors in the handbook or templates, open an issue.
- **Template improvements:** Submit PRs with improved starter templates.
- **Diagram contributions:** High-quality architecture diagrams are welcome.
